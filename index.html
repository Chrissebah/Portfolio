About Me

Frontend Developer

Hello, I am a 23 year old junior frontend developer from Trondheim, Norway. I spent 12 years of my life living in England where I learned to speak, read and write English. I have always had a passion for technology and decided to persue IT when I moved back to Norway. I completed my apprenticeship and now soon my Frontend course at Noroff.

Throughout the 2 years at Noroff I have learnt how to use HTML, CSS and Javascript. I have also learnt to implement the use of REST API into my work requiring the use of JWT to authorise users. I have recently started to use REACT and Bootstrap to simplify my workload and further my knowledge. For the most part I have not focused on the visual aspects of the websites but rather the functionality, but making good looking websites is a goal for future projects.
